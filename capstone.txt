ivsd
Interventricular Septal thickness in Diastole (cm)
Thickness of the wall between left and right ventricles.
Typical values:
Normal: ~0.6 – 1.0 cm
Thickened (hypertrophy): > 1.1 cm
Hypertrophy Levels:
Mild: 1.1–1.3 cm.
Moderate: 1.4–1.6 cm.
Severe: >= 1.7 cm.

lvpwd
Left Ventricular Posterior Wall thickness in Diastole (cm)
Typical values:
Normal: ~0.6 – 1.0 cm
Abnormal: > 1.1 cm
Hypertrophy Levels:
| Condition      | IVSD / LVPWD     |
| -------------- | ---------------- |
| Normal         | ≤ 1.0 cm         |
| Mild LVH       | 1.1 – 1.3 cm     |
| Moderate LVH   | 1.4 – 1.6 cm     |
| **Severe LVH** | **≥ 1.7–1.8 cm** |

lvidd(echo)
Left Ventricular Internal Diameter in Diastole (cm)
Size of the LV cavity when the heart is relaxed.
Typical values:
Normal adult: ~3.5 – 5.9 cm
Dilated LV: > 6.0 cm


If lvidd is above threshold
Dilated left ventricle
Encoded as:
dlv = 1


LV Mass Index (LVMI)
LV mass indexed to body surface area (BSA)
with sex-specific thresholds
LV Mass formula
LV mass ∝ (IVSD + LVIDD + LVPWD)^3 − (LVIDD)^3
